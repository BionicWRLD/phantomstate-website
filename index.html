ABOUT PHANTOM STATE

Experience the ultimate FiveM roleplay server with aesthetics inspired by Miami Vice & Night City. Immerse yourself in a world of luxury, crime, and endless possibilities inside Phantom State.

Luxury Transportation

Drive exclusive cars, Fly high-end aircraft, and live life in the Fast Lane. From Pegassi to Private Jets, experience the high life in Phantom State.

Command Your Empire

Build your own criminal empire or a respectable business. From nightclubs and mechanics to drug cartels and bank robbers, the choice is yours in this unique open-world experience.

Top Notch Community

Join a community full of people who are committed to continue providing entertainment and great roleplay. With our active staff and community, members are held accountable for their violations.

STAPLES OF PHANTOM STATE

Discover what makes Phantom State the Next Generation of your FiveM Roleplay Experience.

Welcoming Community

Phantom State welcomes all players with any amount experience 18 and up. Our city has a no-pay-to-win policy, allowing everyone the chance to get to the top. Although you can spend real money to get some VIP Vehicles, these vehicles are purely unique in design and have loads of cosmetics!

Luxury Properties

Have your own personal penthouse, mansion, or villa. With our Property System, you can have any house with any interior!

Hottest VIP Selection

Our massive VIP Vehicle Collection has something for everyone and its for the right price. All profits go right back into the server, bettering your experience without breaking the bank!

Elite Entertainment Options

From Bowling and Billiards to practicing your aim in VR Zombies or Paintball. Phantom State has plenty of things for you and your friends to do to party or kill time!

Extended Clothing Selection

We have one of the best Custom Clothing Packs on FiveM. Our pack has so much, you will never run out of new outfits to wear! Pair it with our Clothing Menu that allows you to preview images of the item beforehand, and you will be UNSTOPPABLE!

Balanced Roleplay

At Phantom State, we strive to balance all 3 aspects of Roleplay (Gov, Civ, and Gang/Criminal). Each "Department" of RP has plenty of things to do to keep you busy, increasing fairness for whatever path you may choose!

CODE OF THE PHANTOM

Essential rules for maintaining order and quality within Phantom State.

General Code

  • Respect all syndicate members and leadership
  • No exploitation of system vulnerabilities
  • Maintain character integrity at all times
  • Follow syndicate hierarchy and commands

Roleplay Standards

  • Maintain immersive character development
  • No breaking character without permission
  • Respect ongoing storylines and plots
  • No powergaming or meta-gaming allowed

Criminal Operations

  • Honor criminal hierarchy and territories
  • No random violence without purpose
  • Maintain realistic criminal behavior
  • Respect law enforcement roleplay

VIP Standards

  • Maintain premium roleplay quality
  • Lead by example in all interactions
  • Support new members and storylines
  • Contribute to server development
